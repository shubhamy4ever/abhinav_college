Study of the Learner's psychology teaches us that although the child is growing up  every day, his mental, emotional & to some extent  physical needs remain more or less the same for a span of about two years.

Thus to simplify the process of teaching and to organize the learning environment effectively, we need to treat the students to adjacent standards in similar ways, train them in similar skills and judge them with similar criteria. Hence the learning groups are arranged accordingly.
