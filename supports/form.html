Problems in accessing this Google Form? Check the following?

1. Google Forms require a Google login to Sign-in. Since we work a lot with Google forms at Abhinav, it would be worth your while to create a google account.

2. Sometimes the Google Forms application can be slow especially if you have a slow internet like less than 240 Kbps. You could either change to a faster computer or phone network or simply wait a few hours until the ISP in your area manages the traffic block

3. If any particular answer box is not accepting your answer, please check if you have used the proper characters like '@' in the Email field or that there should be no spaces in a date or phone no, etc.
