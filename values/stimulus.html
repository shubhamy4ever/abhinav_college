An Abhinav Interschool & Inter-College Festival of Learning

For schedule of events and enrollment look below

I invite you to participate in the 11th edition of Stimulus – our annual Learning Festival. As each year, we take a theme and this year its “Global Understanding” in keeping with UNESCO’s International Year concept. We have thematic activities and competitions planned for students of Std I to XII. These include exhibitions and Quizzes and many other competitions based on the theme.

The theme is highlighted by the International Geographical Union to bring people to reflect upon the vast diversity of ethnic groups and habitats on our planet and the different ways in which we face common problems related to our day-to-day life, culture and survival. It is essential for a better world that every human being understands the commonality of the human condition and the futility of conflict. Such can be brought about through nurture at a very young age and hence our Stimulus.

The exhibitions will be open to public from 8 am to noon on the 31st December 2016 and everyone is invited to visit. We especially would love to have schools visit us as every year with as many students they can manage since the entire focus of this event is upon the young minds. The competitions will be held during this time and the finals will be held on stage between 12:30 pm and 2 pm, followed by the valediction ceremony until about 3:30 pm. Student delegations from all schools/groups should enroll by the 27th Dec, 2016 so that we can convey them the time slots for their events, in time. There is no fee for participation or visit.
